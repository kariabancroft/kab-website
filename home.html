Kari Bancroft

Kari is Director of Education and Technology at Ada Developers Academy where she works as a manager, mentor and developer. Kari serves on the Board of Directors for RailsBridge where she works to empower volunteers, work on strategic initiatives and organize workshops in Seattle. Kari previously worked for many years as a Software Engineer, was an Instructor for a Girls Who Code and has done a great deal of volunteer work with youth.
When she's not teaching or writing code, Kari is probably playing with her dog Kylo, dancing around the house or writing songs about snacks.
